## baseline

All stats based on Level 150 unboosted creatures — no server multipliers or custom imprints. Assumes working knowledge of basic ARK mechanics.

## tier-list

Water Tiers (Non-Progression): Pacific — Alpha equivalent | Atlantic — Apex equivalent

## getting-started

Build core crafting structures and prepare for early hunts.

### Core Crafting Structures

**Primal Forge** — Smelts metal and alloys. Crafts advanced ingots (Copper, Tin). Required for Zinc Ore.

**Primal Cauldron** — Brews custom kibbles, potions, dyes, and consumables.

**Primal Bench** — Primary station for modded gear, kibble, and boss item crafting.

### Loot System

All loot rolls randomized stat bonuses — armor, weapons, tools, saddles, rings, and dino totems. Sources: Loot Bosses, Quests, Wild dino kills. Higher tiers provide more stat slots and stronger values. Quality ranges from Common through Ancient.

## alpha

Proximity buff increases damage and resistance for nearby dinos.

> STRUCTURE DAMAGE: Wood

### Gear Up

**Weapons** — Primal Spear, Bow, Tranq Arrows

**Utility** — Primal Parachute, Primal Grappling Gun

**Alpha Blood** — Used to craft Alpha-tier tranq ammo

**Alpha Hide** — Used for essential items such as the Primal Bed

> Alpha tames provide the Alpha Buff — doubles damage and resistance for all nearby tamed dinos. Keep one nearby during any boss fight.

### First Tames

Once Alpha Kibble is available from egg farms, prioritize: Pteranodons for travel and scouting. Parasaurs, Trikes, or Stegos for berry farming.

### Boss: The Alpha Boss

Primary progression gate of the Alpha tier. Defeating it unlocks Apex Kibble and the Primal Incubator.

**Preparation** — Craft summon at Primal Bench. Use a secure enclosed arena. Bring saddled dinos and Alpha Health Potions.

**Phase 1** — Defeat the Alpha Boss.

**Phase 2** — Immediate respawn as Skeletal Alpha Boss. Both phases required for full rewards.

**Rewards** — Alpha Boss Skull | Unlocks Apex Kibble | Unlocks Primal Incubator

### Skeletal and Boss Variants

Skeletal variants require Alpha/Apex Boss Kibble to tame and drop Keratin and Blood. Boss variants are stronger, drop Element, and can be bred.

## apex

Increased strength and durability over Alpha tier.

> STRUCTURE DAMAGE: Stone

### Bloodrage Mechanic

Apex creatures gain Bloodrage during combat — each kill increases damage output and provides health regeneration over time.

### Advancing to Apex

Target Rexes, Spinos, and other high-DPS Apex dinos. Replace weaker Alpha tames with higher-stat Apex variants.

### Boss: The Apex Troll

First boss encounter in this tier. Defeating it unlocks Elemental Kibble.

> ⚠️ WARNING: Boss fights apply a Grounded Debuff — disables flight, abilities, and mounting. No fliers.

### Apex Feather Farm

Establish a reliable feather source before progressing — required for mid- to late-game crafting and kibble. Sources: Argentavis, Dodo, Terror Bird, Archaeopteryx. Apex Dodos are the easiest starting option.

## elemental

Exploit elemental strengths and weaknesses — team composition is critical.

> STRUCTURE DAMAGE: Stone

> Any Elemental Blood can be used interchangeably — blood from any elemental type satisfies requirements calling for Elemental Blood.

### Elemental Kibble

Elemental creatures drop their respective cores (e.g. Volcanic Core, Hydro Core). In the Primal Cauldron: 1 Core → 10 Essence. Elemental Essence crafts Elemental Kibble.

### Elemental Matchups

![Elemental Wheel](images/elemental_wheel_nemesis.png)

Storm — Strong vs Hydro | Weak to Earth | Mobile power generation, scales range with melee

Volcanic — Strong vs Frost | Weak to Hydro | Cook meat in inventory, mobile forge, temperature resist

Hydro — Strong vs Volcanic | Weak to Storm | Water enhancement, store water, reduce O2 drain nearby

Frost — Strong vs Nature | Weak to Volcanic | Food preservation like a fridge, temperature resist

Nature — Strong vs Earth | Weak to Frost | Crop growth boost, healing AoE, 150% greenhouse effect

Earth — Strong vs Storm | Weak to Nature | Crafting speed boost, rock shield for nearby allies

> ⚠️ WARNING: Nature dinos provide a 150% greenhouse effect that REPLACES existing greenhouse bonuses. Do not use near a full greenhouse (300%) — it will reduce efficiency.

### Base Pairing Strategy

Storm + Hydro — Power generation + water supply

Earth + Nature — Faster crafting + improved crop production

Frost + Volcanic — Full temperature control across all environments

### Boss: Elemental Kaiju Bosses

Can be crafted at the Primal Bench and challenged in any order.

**Preparation** — Fully saddled and healed tames. Stock Alpha and Nature Potions. Build a team of evolved Titans with high Health and Melee.

**Rewards** — Boss Skull | Elemental Fruit Seeds | Elemental Boss Essence

## plasma-ghost-plague

> STRUCTURE DAMAGE: Stone

### Type Triangle

### Plasma

Creatures composed of unstable plasma energy. Plasma beats Ghost. Immune to projectiles. Weak against Plague.

New Tools: Plasma Netgun (traps Ghosts) | GhostBuster 9000 (KO tool)

**Boss: Plasma Boss** — Beware the shield, keep your distance and dodge all attacks. Can summon Minions similar to the vanilla Overseer fight.

> ⚠️ WARNING: Huge health pool. Come fully stocked on potions.

### Ghost

Spectral aquatic creatures with powerful nighttime advantages. Immune to projectiles and normal tranqs. Can fly out of water. Extremely strong at night.

**Taming** — Plasma Netgun to trap | GhostBuster 9000 to KO

**Boss: Ghost Boss** — Flyers only. Must be fought with flying creatures. Ground tames not viable. Weak to Plasma. Ghosts strongest at night.

> ⚠️ WARNING: Non-flyer strategies will fail. Bring strong fliers — boss has a large health pool.

### Plague

Creatures influenced by virulent corruption. Plague beats Plasma. Spreads a virus on contact — short-duration damage-over-time debuff.

**Boss: Plague Boss** — Defeating unlocks Bionic Kibble.

## bionic

> STRUCTURE DAMAGE: Stone

### Boss Chain

Defeat bosses to acquire both Bionic Skulls. Then craft the Bionic God Scroll at the Primal Bench to unlock the Scion of Darkness.

**Victory Conditions** — High DPS for boss phase | Fast tower-breakers for shield phase | Strong mobility to dodge plasma fire

**Rewards** — Bionic Boss Skull | Bionic Boss Essence | Unlocks Primal Bionic Forge

### Primal Bionic Forge

Unlocked by defeating the Bionic God. Only station that can refine Mithril Ore → Mithril Ingots and Gold Ore → Gold Ingots.

**Power Requirements** — Storm Essence | Volcanic Essence | Or a nearby Storm creature

## celestial-demonic

> STRUCTURE DAMAGE: Tek (AoE attacks)

### The Hunt for Honey

To craft Demonic and Celestial Kibble, hunt Demonic Bees and Celestial Bees in the wild.

> ⚠️ WARNING: These bees hit hard — bring your best gear or prepare to respawn.

**Required** — Strong Bionic-tier tame | Fabricated Sniper Rifle | High-tier Bionic+ Tranqs

**Two paths** — Full Assault (kill directly) or Tranq and Finish. Both yield 2 Honey per bee.

### Tame Your First Bees

KO and tame Demonic and Celestial Bees. Convert them into a Beehive via radial menu and insert Celestial or Demonic Flowers for passive honey production.

> Tame as many variants as possible — each has unique abilities, and some are game-changers.

### Boss: Demonic and Celestial Bosses

**Prep** — Bring strongest Bionic/Demonic/Celestial creatures with high-quality saddles and imprints. Stockpile Alpha Health Potions and Nature Buffs. Use Alpha Buffs mid-fight.

**Rewards** — Demonic / Celestial Boss Skull | Boss Essence | Celestial and Demonic Seeds (for Godly Kibble)

## godly

Health potions recommended due to high stats and projectile damage.

Six Godly Bosses roam the map — only one of each at a time. You will be notified when one spawns. On death each boss summons a tameable version of itself.

### Boss: Nemesis — The Godly Boss

Collect tokens from all six Godly Bosses to summon Nemesis — the ultimate early-game fight. Conquer it to complete the early-game arc.

**Rewards** — Godly Potions | Max XP Potions | Godly Boss Essence | Godly Boss Skull

### Creatures

## loot-bosses

Rare enemies that spawn naturally in the wild across all tiers. Project a red beam into the sky — when attacked, the beam disappears to signal engagement. Drops go into inventory, not on the ground.

**Saddles** — Crafted saddles are always Primitive. Loot bosses are the primary source of higher-quality saddles with randomized stats. Can drop finished saddles and blueprints.

**Tame Helpers** — Basic Tame Helper (x10) craftable in Primal Cauldron. Stronger versions are exclusive loot boss drops. Alpha and Nature potions craftable; Plasma, Celestial, and Godly potions are exclusive drops.

**Quests** — Common Resource Quests triggered by killing creatures. High-Level Quests dropped by Imposter Loot Bosses (Alpha, Apex, Elemental, Bionic).

> Quest scrolls must be consumed from a dino's inventory. Quest UI is only visible while riding that specific dino.

## loot-system

All dropped loot rolls randomized stat bonuses — similar to MMO loot systems.

Stat slots by tier: Alpha 2 slots Low | Apex 3 slots Medium | Elemental+ More slots Higher values | Godly Most slots Highest values

Loot Quality: Common → Uncommon → Rare → Epic → Legendary → Divine → Ancient

Ancient only obtainable through quests. Divine is the new high-end tier above Legendary.

**Unique Weapons** — Zeus Spear (throws lightning bolts where it lands) | Ragnarok Spear (unleashes nukes or meteors on impact)

## resource-ores

### Nickel Ore

Source: Resource Rock Golem

Hover above a Resource Rock Golem with a flying mount to bait it into spawning Minions. Defeat and harvest Minions using a Modded Dino (Apex Rex recommended). Kill and harvest the Golem — Modded Tools are bugged, use Modded Dinos.

> Look for Resource Rock Golems that show "Can be tamed with Modded Ore" — tame one and use its ability to spawn your own Minions for unlimited Nickel.

**Taming the Golem** — Griffin hover and shoot straight down with Rocket Launcher aimed at the head. Or trap and use a Cannon for Torpor. Captain Reptile's Cannon mod can help.

### Starfire Metal / Mithril / Gold

Source: DemiGod StarFire Golem

When fighting a Resource Rock Golem there is a 25% chance it respawns as a DemiGod StarFire Golem. During the fight it periodically summons meteors — harvest these for Starfire Metal, Mithril Ore, and Gold Ore. Each meteor lasts 7 minutes.

> The meteor ability fires every 3 minutes. Keep the DemiGod alive to farm multiple waves — the most efficient method for gathering these ores.

**Refining** — Mithril and Gold Ore can only be smelted in the Primal Bionic Forge. Requires Storm Essence, Volcanic Essence, or a nearby Storm creature.

### Rice / Kelp / Sushi

Use a Berry Gatherer (Trike, Bronto, Parasaur) to collect Rice and Seaweed seeds from Berry Bushes. Plant in greenhouse. For Prime Fish Meat target Basilosaurus, Deinosuchus, Mosasaurus, or Spine Croc with Pickaxe or Chainsaw.

> ⚠️ WARNING: Prime Fish Meat does NOT stack — plan inventory space accordingly.

Combine Prime Fish Meat, Rice, and Kelp in the Primal Cauldron. Cauldron holds 50 items max.

## charms

All enhancers roll a value within a range when obtained — two items of the same type can have different strength levels.

### Player Ring System

Rings are modular equipment with insertable charms. Ring tiers: Common → Uncommon → Rare → Epic → Legendary → Godly. Common rings start with 1 charm slot; each tier adds capacity.

### Enhancer Types

XP — Player and Dino — Increases experience gain

Weight — Player and Dino — Increases carrying capacity

Speed — Player and Dino — Increases movement speed

Healing — Player and Dino — Gradual health regeneration

Dodge — Player and Dino — Chance to evade incoming damage

Timestop — Player and Dino — Stops time briefly when triggered

Gravity — Player and Dino — Reduces gravity for higher jumps

Fall Damage — Player and Dino — Reduces fall damage

Crafting — Player only — Reduces crafting time and cost

Heat — Player only — Reduces heat effects

Baby — Dino only — Affects baby growth and maturation

## titans

### How to Tame a Titan

1. Defeat the Elemental Boss — Unlocks the Elemental Incubator Engram and drops the Boss Skull (required for Whelpling recipe)

2. Hunt a Wild Elemental Youngling — Cannot be crafted or summoned. On kill automatically drops an Elemental Heart into your inventory.

3. Place Incubator strategically — Keep one at base for recipes (DO NOT hatch from it) and one in an open area for the actual hatch.

4. Craft the Whelpling — Spawns immediately as a wild creature. KO and feed matching Elemental Kibble.

> ⚠️ WARNING: Younglings hit HARDER than Elemental Bosses. Come fully geared with your best tames. Do NOT summon a Whelpling inside your base — it will destroy structures.

### Titan Evolution

Whelpling — Baby/juvenile version

Youngling — Use evolution item in inventory. Similar strength to wild variant.

Adult Titan — Gains ALL special attacks from boss variant. Ideal for Elemental boss fights.

## faq

**How do dinos spawn?** Modded creatures piggyback from vanilla dino spawns. A vanilla Argent spawning might result in an Alpha Argent or Apex Argent instead.

**How do I get unfertilized eggs for kibble?** Set layegginterval to 0.3 in GAME INI. Ensure dinos are mate boosted (1 male + females). Disable mating (unfertilized eggs needed). Use an OviRaptor with egg-boost buff. Stay in render range unless using a mod.

**Why aren't modded dinos spawning?** Move the mod to 1st/top of mod order then perform a dino wipe.

**Can we take modded dinos into boss arenas?** If their vanilla counterpart can enter the modded version can also. Note: Due to size increases at higher tiers some dinos become too large to enter the Tek Cave (e.g. Elemental Rexes).

**What are Colossal and Pygmy traits?** Both have faster movement and increased melee damage. Colossal — Higher base health and melee, suited for tanking. Pygmy — 10% chance to dodge incoming attacks, effective in long fights. Can be applied via trait items or appear randomly through breeding.

**What are the new dino traits?** Nuclear — Builds an internal timer when aggressive or taking damage. Detonates in a nuclear explosion at zero. When tamed triggers below 10% health. Volatile — Explodes on death, both wild and tamed. Nightmare — Doubled stats during nighttime. Nighthunter — Extremely aggressive at night with increased aggro range. Chunky — Increased harvest health, yields more materials when harvested.

**How does the quality ranking system work?** Common → Uncommon → Rare → Epic → Legendary → Divine → Ancient. Divine is the new high-end tier above Legendary. Ancient only obtainable through quests.
