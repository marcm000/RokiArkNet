# Ark Supreme

> Mod in active testing. Guide updated as content is verified.

## Tier List

```
Toxic
 └─ Berserk
        └─ Necrotic
                └─ Prime
                        └─ Nuclear | Vampire
                                └─ Wardens
                                        └─ Hellfire
                                                └─ DarkGod
                                                        └─ Primordials | Spectral Wardens | Corrupted DarkGods
```

> Endgame note: Primordials, Spectral Wardens, and Corrupted DarkGods are equivalent in strength.

## Toxic

Killing and harvesting Toxic dinos yields Toxic Blood, used to craft kibble for taming Toxic dinos. Toxic dinos perform a torpor-dealing attack on alternate attack.

## Berserk

Kill and harvest Berserk dinos for Berserk Blood, used to craft kibble for taming Berserk dinos.
